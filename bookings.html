Important booking information

To make your visit as smooth and enjoyable as possible, please take a moment to review the following details before you book.

Session duration

All sessions at Whiskers' Cafe are one hour long, giving you plenty of time to relax and interact with our cats.

Opening hours

Our cafe is open from 1pm to 9pm daily, ready to welcome you for a delightful experience.

Age restrictions

Children under 13 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian to ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone.
